WHO AM I?

I'm a Mumbai based photographer with a goal - to document real experiences as they unfold. I have trekked up inclines, trudged through mud, and been soaked by downpours all in the pursuit of life-affirming photographs. My mission is to capture the wonders of this beautiful planet and celebrate the differences we all are fortunate enough to experience.

MY PHILOSOPHY

I am the go with the flow kind of a person. Making sure you are completely comfortable is my top priority. I focus on evoking emotion rather than twisting you into stiff poses. I want to see real laughter, happy tears, soft cuddles. After all, this is where the real magical memories are born. My mission is to photograph the one and only, unique you in the best possible light.

SERVICES

I specialize in nature photography but will take on any fun portrait sessions too. My services don’t just end in digitals; photos are meant to be printed! I make shopping for products easy with hand-selected packages. I help each one of my clients decide the best way to display and protect their precious memories.
